# Revenue Model — Maxify Procurement Agent

## Equipment Margin Model

Maxify adds a configurable margin on top of wholesale supplier pricing. This margin is transparent to the contractor — they see a single "Maxify Price" that includes the margin.

### Margin Structure

| Category | Target Margin | Rationale |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Solar Panels | 3-8% | Commodity market, price-sensitive. Compete on volume. |
| Inverters | 5-10% | More complex, less price comparison. Higher margin opportunity. |
| Batteries | 5-10% | Growing category, less price transparency. |
| Racking/BOS | 8-15% | Fragmented market, highest margin. Contractors care less about specific brand. |
| EV Chargers | 8-12% | Emerging add-on category. |

### Volume Tiers

| Monthly Volume | Margin Adjustment |
|---------------|-------------------|
| < $10K | Standard margins |
| $10K - $50K | -1% across categories |
| $50K - $200K | -2% across categories |
| > $200K | Custom negotiated |

## Subscription Tiers

Equipment margins subsidize SaaS subscription costs:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Equipment Margin | Quotes/Month | Suppliers | Features |
|------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Starter** | $0 | 10-15% | 5 | 2 | Basic quoting, email notifications |
| **Pro** | $49 | 5-10% | Unlimited | All | Full analytics, SMS alerts, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | 3-5% | Unlimited | All + custom | API access, white-label, dedicated account manager |

## Competitive Pricing Analysis

### vs. OpenSolar
- OpenSolar: Free software → charges hardware partners for marketplace listing + takes commission on finance referrals
- Maxify: Low-cost SaaS + moderate equipment margin → dual revenue stream
- Advantage: Maxify contractors pay less total cost because margin is lower than OpenSolar's marketplace fees

### vs. Traditional Distribution
- Traditional: Contractors call 3-4 suppliers, get quotes in 1-2 days each
- Maxify: One click, parallel quotes, responses in hours
- Value proposition: Time savings alone worth the margin premium

## Revenue Projections (Conservative)

### Year 1 Assumptions
- 50 active contractors by Q4 2026
- Average $15K equipment per project
- Average 3 projects/month per contractor
- 7% average margin

### Year 1 Revenue
- Equipment margin: 50 × 3 × $15,000 × 7% = $157,500/month
- SaaS subscriptions: 30 Pro ($49) + 15 Starter ($0) + 5 Enterprise ($200 avg) = $2,470/month
- **Total Year 1 annual revenue: ~$1.9M** (mostly equipment margin)
